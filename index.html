About Sonufive LLC

Two shops. One family. A decade in.

Sonufive LLC is the holding company behind two North Texas liquor stores. We're not a chain — we're a family operation that's been stocking shelves and remembering regulars for more than ten years.

Our story

It started with one store, one family, and a lot of shelves to fill.

We opened our doors in 2014 with a simple idea: a neighborhood liquor store should feel like one. Not fluorescent, not anonymous, not someone trying to sell you the most expensive thing on the wall.

Ten-plus years later, we run two stores in North Texas. The names on the signs are different, but the approach is the same: helpful staff, honest recommendations, and a deep bench of bottles — from the $12 Tuesday red to the limited-run bourbon you've been trying to track down.

We're proud of what we've built. More than that, we're grateful to the regulars who built it with us.

How we run things

Three things we've stuck to for ten years.

01

Know the shelf

Our staff actually tastes what we sell. If we recommend it, there's a reason. If we don't, we'll say so.

02

Stock what locals ask for

Our shelves reflect our neighborhood. Special request? Tell us — there's a good chance we can get it in by next week.

03

Never push the price

We'd rather steer you toward the $22 bottle you'll love than the $85 bottle you won't. That's how you end up with regulars.

Get in touch

Give us a ring or stop on in.

We don't take online orders — but we answer the phone, and we'll hold a bottle for you if you ask.
